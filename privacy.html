Privacy Policy

MeowMeow7 Poster is a personal automation tool used solely by the account owner to publish video content that the owner has personally created and approved to their own TikTok account.

Data We Access

We access basic profile information (user.info.basic) and use the video publishing scope (video.upload, video.publish) only to post the account owner's own content to the account owner's own TikTok account.

Data We Do Not Do

We do not collect, store, sell, or share any TikTok user data with third parties. No data from other TikTok users is accessed.

Data Storage

Access tokens are stored securely in a local, private environment file and are never shared publicly.

Contact

For questions about this policy, contact the account owner directly through TikTok.
